Hi, I’m Josh (Zhuo Shi). I was born in 2015. I am a pupil now, but my dad and myself don’t see me just as a little boy and don’t put any restriction upon my learning, so we have learned a lot of things together.

I am:

  • A mathematics enthusiast who is always trying to explain or model everything in math.

  • A bilingual (I speak Chinese and English), and I am trying to be a polyglot now: I’m learning Espanöl and Deutsch.

  • An amateur real cuber - I had collected all the cubes used in WCA events (actually, much more than that) and got the ability to solve all of them. But I don’t do blind solving and FMC very well.

  • An amateur MineCraft player, who mostly stays in creative mode.
